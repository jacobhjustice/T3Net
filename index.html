What is T
3
.Net?

T3Net is a web client I created as a fun little project based off a varient of Tic Tac Toe that I was taught in highschool. The game is pretty simple. The board is a 3x3 board of squares, each made up of 3x3 cells. Wherever you move determines where the other player is allowed to move.
Whichever cell you play in, will be the square that your opponent will choose from in their turn.
Get 3 cells in a row within a square, then that square becomes yours!
If the corresponding square is filled up, then your opponent will be able to play in any open cell!
You win the game by getting 3 squares in a row! You have to think ahead for which squares you need in order to win! If you lose a square, don't fret! You can always use it to your advantage by forcing your opponent to play in a corresponding cell (since it will give you free pick over the board)!
